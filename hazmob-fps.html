Hazmob FPS

Hazmob FPS: Online Shooter is a multiplayer FPS game that pits you against other players in intense battles across various game modes. Dive into a quick battle to jump right into the action!

How to Play

Immerse Yourself in Battle

Hazmob FPS: Online Shooter is perfect for skilled FPS enthusiasts. The game boasts stunning 3D graphics and a smooth, intuitive control system, allowing for quick and tactical maneuvers. The maps are designed for high-speed, close-quarters combat, making reflexes and skill crucial to your success.

Upgrade Your Weapons

With an extensive arsenal of over 55 customizable weapons, including SMGs, assault rifles, shotguns, sniper rifles, and LMGs, you can tailor your loadout to suit your playstyle. As you progress, unlock new weapons and attachments. Customize your guns with scopes, skins, keychains, and upgrade their abilities to enhance your performance in battle.

Earn More Money for Gear

In addition to weapons, you can equip clothing items to boost your character’s base stats. Earn in-game currency from battles to purchase these items. Complete daily and weekly missions to increase your earnings and acquire better gear.

Create Custom Matches

Hazmob FPS: Online Shooter allows you to create custom games for private matches with friends or competitive battles against rival clans. The competitive rank system tracks the best clans, displaying their trophies and XP scores, adding an extra layer of competition.

Play Various Game Modes

Ready for instant action? Click quick battle to join a random game on one of the 8 available maps. Alternatively, explore a range of other game modes, including:

  • Team Deathmatch: Two teams compete to reach the kill limit first.
  • Search and Destroy: Teams alternate between attacking and defending objectives.
  • Capture the Flag: Steal the enemy's flag and return it to your base.
  • Domination: Control designated points on the map to earn points over time.
  • Free for All: Every player for themselves; the highest score wins.
  • Elimination: Eliminate all opponents to win the round.
  • Gun Race: Progress through a series of weapons by scoring kills.

Whether you’re aiming for fast-paced action or strategic team play, Hazmob FPS: Online Shooter offers a variety of modes to suit your preferences. Gear up, join the battle, and prove your skills in this thrilling FPS experience!
